About Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a warrior king of the Maratha Empire. He was born in 1627 in the Indian state of Maharashtra and founded the Maratha Empire in 1674. He is considered one of the greatest warriors in Indian history.

Shivaji Maharaj fought against the Mughal Empire and the Adil Shahi Sultanate to establish his kingdom. He was known for his military tactics, guerrilla warfare, and diplomacy. He was a true visionary and a leader of the people.

"Freedom is the life breath of nations."

- Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj
